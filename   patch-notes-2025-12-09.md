**Patch Notes Update - December 2025**

---

### **New Features and Enhancements**

- **AI Standardization Update:** Big Tech and Linux Foundation have collaborated to introduce the "Agentic AI Foundation" in the latest patch. This update standardizes AI agents with new protocols like MCP, AGENTS.md, and the mysterious "goose" system. Players can now expect more consistent AI behavior across different platforms. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/ai/2025/12/big-tech-joins-forces-with-linux-foundation-to-standardize-ai-agents/))

- **Lunar Droid Companion:** NASA astronauts on the Moon now have a new companion! The Lunar Outpost Rover has been added to the game, allowing astronauts to work alongside a robot for the first time on a celestial body other than Earth. This feature enhances exploration capabilities and adds a fun dynamic to Moon missions. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/space/2025/12/lunar-outpost-rover-to-study-lunar-dust-alongside-artemis-astronauts-on-moon/))

- **Gesture Controls for Pixel Watch 4:** Google has reintroduced gesture controls, but this time it's exclusive to the Pixel Watch 4. Players can now dismiss or select items with a flick of the wrist, adding a layer of cool to your watch gameplay. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2025/12/google-is-reviving-wearable-gesture-controls-but-only-for-the-pixel-watch-4/))

- **Smart-ish Ring:** Pebble, the maker of the beloved smartwatch, has released the Index 01, a smart-ish ring for under $100. While not fully smart, it offers some smart functionalities like audio note capture. A budget-friendly addition for tech enthusiasts in the game. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2025/12/resurrected-pebble-maker-announces-a-kind-of-smart-ring-for-capturing-audio-notes/))

- **Mars Mission Rationale:** A new rationale for sending astronauts to Mars has been added to the game lore, inspired by a major scientific report. This update adds depth to the Mars mission storyline, making it more engaging for players. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/space/2025/12/in-a-major-new-report-scientists-build-rationale-for-sending-astronauts-to-mars/))

- **Golden Dome Justification:** The rationale for the Golden Dome project now includes a reference to a Hollywood film for inspiration. This quirky addition aims to make the project more relatable and less like a nuclear war deterrent, because if it works in movies, why not in games? (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/space/2025/12/asked-why-we-need-golden-dome-the-man-in-charge-points-to-a-hollywood-film/))

- **Turner Prize Winner:** Artist Nnena Kalu has won the Turner Prize for her unique sculptures and drawings, becoming the first learning-disabled artist to receive this honor in-game. This update celebrates diversity in the art world within our virtual realm. (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yqvz9wljqo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Google Smart Glasses 2.0:** Google is planning a comeback with smart glasses in 2026, following the flop of Google Glass. This feature is teased in the current patch, promising a more refined and hopefully successful tech accessory in the game. (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyx83n00k6o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

### **Bug Fixes and Balance Changes**

- **FTC Firing Approval:** The Supreme Court has patched in an approval for Trump’s ability to fire FTC Democrats, adjusting the balance of power within the game's political simulation. Conservative justices now have a stronger influence in this aspect. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2025/12/supreme-court-appears-likely-to-approve-trumps-firing-of-ftc-democrat/))

- **Legal Defense Patch:** A new legal patch clarifies that "Because Trump said to" is no longer a valid in-game defense. This change aims to reduce arbitrary and capricious decisions within the game's legal system. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2025/12/trumps-order-blocking-wind-development-thrown-out-by-court/))

- **Amazon Protections:** Post-COP30, Brazil has weakened Amazon protections in-game, introducing a choice between environment or economy for players, reflecting real-world trade-offs. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/science/2025/12/days-after-cop30-brazil-weakened-amazon-safeguards/))

- **Roman Concrete Recipe:** The latest patch includes the confirmed recipe for Roman concrete, enhancing construction gameplay with historical accuracy. Players can now build with the durability of ancient Rome. (Source: [Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com/science/2025/12/study-confirms-romans-used-hot-mixing-to-make-concrete/))

- **MI5 Involvement:** MI5's role in the IRA spy Stakeknife scenario has been expanded, providing deeper espionage gameplay mechanics with more realistic security service involvement. (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2g05g3r7xo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **ECHR Migration Talks:** Major talks on ECHR migration rules have started, affecting international relations gameplay. This could lead to new diplomatic missions or conflicts. (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2g03gv2z6o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Flu Outbreak:** A mutated flu virus is now circulating in-game, prompting players to consider vaccine purchases.
