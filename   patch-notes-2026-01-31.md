**Patch Notes v2026.1.1 - Reality Update**

---

**General Fixes & Enhancements:**

- **Health & Wellness:** 
  - **Coffee Over LSD:** Coffee consumption now has a +10% effectiveness boost against depression compared to microdosing LSD. Caffeine buffs have been rebalanced; placebo effect is now recognized as a legitimate strategy in mental health management. (Source: [Coffee vs. Microdosing](https://arstechnica.com/health/2026/01/placebo-outperforms-lsd-microdosing-for-depression/))

- **Entertainment & Technology:**
  - **8K TV Resolution:** Due to lack of content and minimal gameplay enhancement, 8K resolution has been downgraded to "Unnecessary Feature". Players are now directed to invest in more practical upgrades. (Source: [8K TV Reality Check](https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2026/01/lg-joins-the-rest-of-the-world-accepts-that-people-dont-want-8k-tvs/))

- **Political Drama:**
  - **Andrew's Involvement:** Added a new questline involving Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor, requiring him to testify before the US Congress. New character interaction with Keir Starmer, who has issued this quest. (Source: [Andrew's Testimony](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgrd7kpg9pwo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
  - **Israeli-Palestinian Conflict:** Implemented a temporary ceasefire event, but beware, random air strikes can occur, affecting gameplay. Both factions can now accuse each other of breaking the truce, adding a layer of complexity to diplomatic relations. (Source: [Gaza Strikes](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c701g1g00gdo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Environment & Survival:**
  - **Ukrainian Winter:** New environmental challenge added: -20C temperatures with energy truce conditions. Players must now manage heat sources more efficiently or face 'Catastrophe' status effect. (Source: [Ukrainian Winter](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c75xg17rr1xo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Cultural Updates:**
  - **In Memoriam - Catherine O'Hara:** Added a tribute event where players can pay respects to Catherine O'Hara. Pedro Pascal and Meryl Streep lead the in-game memorial service. (Source: [Tribute to O'Hara](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czejk8pnk82o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Technology & Innovation:**
  - **SpaceX Satellite Network:** SpaceX now has a quest to launch 1 million satellites. This update introduces 'Orbital Data Centers' for AI power-ups, significantly increasing the game's computational capacity. (Source: [SpaceX Satellites](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cyv5l24mrjmo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Daily Life Improvements:**
  - **Quick-Commerce in India:** New feature: Instant grocery delivery. Players in India can now summon missing curry ingredients within 8 minutes, but be cautious, this might trigger 'Rider Danger' events. (Source: [Quick Commerce](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgx7ykl12go?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Education Policy:**
  - **Smartphone Ban in Schools:** A new policy debate event has been added where parents can vote on banning smartphones in schools. Watch out for the 'Parental Worry' debuff if you oppose. (Source: [Smartphone Ban](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3edydz7deyo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Sports & Entertainment:**
  - **Music Tours:** Harry Styles has introduced a new gameplay mechanic where instead of world tours, artists can now opt for long-term residencies. This affects tour planning strategy for all musicians in-game. (Source: [End of World Tours](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czx3q0qw0npo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Global Events:**
  - **Mine Clearing in Black Sea:** A new high-risk mission for players involves clearing Russian mines in the Black Sea. One wrong move and it's game over, adding intense strategy to underwater operations. (Source: [Mine Clearing](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70lgwprw7ko?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Royal Surprises:**
  - **King at Parkrun:** The King has a new random event where he might surprise joggers at Parkrun events, adding a fun and unexpected royal encounter to the game. (Source: [King's Surprise](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62vx7yx71ro?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

- **Miscellaneous:**
  - **Frozen River Dance Party:** In Kyiv, players can now participate in a dance party on a frozen river, a morale-boosting event amidst power cuts and cold. Adds +5 to community spirit. (Source: [Kyiv Dance Party](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c5yvw9zq5q0o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
  - **Tax Return Deadline:** The tax return deadline event is now live, with phone lines remaining open. Missing the deadline last year has added a 'Procrastination Penalty' debuff to players' stats. (Source: [Tax Deadline](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy10ye2030o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
