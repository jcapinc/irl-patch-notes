**World Update 2026.07 - Patch Notes**

**New Content**
- Added Indian private launch startup as a playable faction. First orbital attempt now available in Early Access. Success rate: “we’ll see.”
- Added Smithsonian Starstruck VR hub. Players may now stroll through stars, exoplanets, and observatories without leaving their basement. Motion sickness debuff remains unchanged.
- Added “Luddite Festival” limited-time event in New York. Gen Z players can now complete daily “rage against Big Tech” quests by touching grass and turning off their phones. Reward: smugness buff.

**Progression & Milestones**
- SpaceX has reached the 1,000-launch milestone. Players who contributed via Starlink subscription will receive a commemorative pixel rocket and a 0.0003 % discount on next month’s bill.
- Three nuclear startups have unlocked Tier 2 reactor designs. Still several years away from actually powering anything meaningful. Classic tech tree.

**Balance Changes**
- All consumer electronics (phones, consoles, laptops) now cost significantly more. This is not a bug; it’s an “AI-driven chip shortage” feature.
- Google DeepMind unionization talks adjusted. Executive engagement reduced from “reluctant” to “actively awkward.”
- England vs Mexico World Cup match kick-off moved to 19:00 BST. No tactical changes, just a scheduling nerf to pub attendance.

**Quality of Life**
- New “walk 30 minutes a day” daily challenge added after NHS reward scheme. Rewards include slightly better cardiovascular stats and the ability to say “I did a thing” in group chats.
- Bone-conduction headphones added to outdoor activity loadouts. Now you can listen to podcasts while still hearing traffic. Survival rate improved.

**Known Issues**
- UK leadership still transitioning. Starmer has exited; Burnham has entered. Patch to resolve “who’s actually in charge” expected later this month.
- Several real-world crimes and disasters remain unresolved. We’re looking into it.

**Notes**
