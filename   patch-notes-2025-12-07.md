**Patch Notes Update - Version 2025.12.12**

---

**General Updates**

- **Dog Behavior Complexity Patch:** 
  - Added: New layer of complexity to canine companions. DNA tests no longer provide full insight into your dog's quirky behaviors. Now, your dog's decision to dig up your garden at 3 AM might be influenced by an intricate web of genetic mysteries. Enjoy the surprise!
  
- **Global Security Strategy Update:**
  - Adjusted: US security strategy now aligns with Russia's vision. No longer does the game flag Russia as a threat, leading to unexpected peace alliances. Players might find this shift in diplomacy intriguing or confusing.

- **Youth Employment Boost:**
  - Implemented: 50,000 new apprenticeship slots added across AI, hospitality, and engineering sectors. Young players now have more career paths to explore in-game, reducing the chance of becoming a 'Forever Intern'.

- **Tax Notification for Online Sellers:**
  - Fixed: Panic no longer necessary when receiving tax messages after selling 30 items on Vinted. New tutorial added to explain online selling tax rules, ensuring players don't flee in terror from their inboxes.

**Sports & Entertainment**

- **Formula 1 Championship Patch:**
  - Achievement Unlocked: Briton Lando Norris has won his first F1 title in Abu Dhabi by finishing third. His tearful victory speech now available in the 'Emotional Moments' section of the game.

- **I'm A Celebrity Winner:**
  - Event Concluded: The 25th series of 'I'm A Celebrity' has crowned its winner in the Australian jungle. Players can now replay the jungle challenges to experience the thrill of survival and fame.

- **Strictly Come Dancing Semi-Final:**
  - Update: Only four couples remain after the musicals week. New dance moves and costumes have been added to reflect this year's competition. Players can now practice these moves in the dance studio.

- **Rugby Ultramarathon Challenge:**
  - Added: Kevin Sinfield's tribute to Rob Burrow with seven ultramarathons in seven days. This new event is now a playable challenge for charity, testing player's endurance and heart.

**Political & Social**

- **Angela Rayner's Political Comeback:**
  - Teaser: Prime Minister Starmer hints at Angela Rayner's return to the cabinet. Players can look forward to new political intrigue and cabinet reshuffle scenarios.

- **Benin Coup Attempt:**
  - Resolved: A coup in Benin was thwarted by loyalist troops. The situation is 'totally under control'. New mission added where players can assist in maintaining national stability.

  - Added: New dialogue option for Trump to criticize Democrats he's pardoned who don't switch parties. This adds a layer of political strategy and betrayal to gameplay.

**Technology & Innovation**

- **Autonomous Vehicles Acceleration:**
  - Update: The accelerator for autonomous vehicles is now fully pressed. Expect faster development cycles and new vehicle types in the transportation section of the game.

- **Coinbase in India:**
  - Expansion: Coinbase begins onboarding users in India again. A new in-game event where players can simulate the crypto market entry, with plans for a fiat on-ramp in the next update.

- **Moore's Law Rescue Mission:**
  - New Quest: Pat Gelsinger aims to save Moore's Law with federal assistance. Players can join this tech saga, aiming to produce silicon wafers by 2028 and have systems online by 2029.
