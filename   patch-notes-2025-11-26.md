**Patch Notes for the Real World - November 2025 Update**

### **General Fixes and Improvements**

- **Solar Power Surge**: Solar energy generation has been buffed significantly, now almost offsetting the increasing energy consumption in the US. Players might notice a slight decrease in their electricity bills. *(Note: Wind power still feeling a bit jealous.)*

- **Taxation Overhaul**: Chancellor Rachel Reeves has introduced several new tax policies:
  - **Income Tax Band Freeze**: The income tax bands have been frozen for an additional three years. Players earning over £100,000 might find this update particularly challenging, akin to a surprise debuff.
  - **Mansion Tax**: A new 'mansion tax' has been implemented for properties valued over £2m. Expect a new status effect: "Wealth Redistribution" when purchasing high-end real estate.
  - **Pay-Per-Mile for EVs**: Electric vehicle owners will now face a 3p per mile tax, while plug-in hybrids get a slightly kinder 1.5p per mile. Consider this a balance adjustment to promote fairness in fuel consumption.

- **Budget Leak Fix**: The Budget Watchdog has patched a significant leak in the system, ensuring that future budget details remain confidential until the official reveal. Stealth missions to gather pre-release info are now less effective.

### **Health and Safety Updates**

- **CDC Leadership**: Ralph Abraham, the new CDC Deputy Director under RFK Jr., now has a preference for "natural immunity" over vaccines. This might introduce a new gameplay mechanic where players choose between natural and medical immunity paths, with varying outcomes.

- **NHS Doctor Suspension**: A doctor has been temporarily removed from the game due to alleged antisemitic posts on social media. This aims to maintain player trust in the medical profession.

- **Drug Consumption Room**: In Glasgow, the Thistle facility now allows for smoking cocaine under medical supervision. This new feature is intended to reduce harm, adding a 'Safe Haven' status effect for drug users.

### **Tech and AI Adjustments**

### **Space and Exploration**
