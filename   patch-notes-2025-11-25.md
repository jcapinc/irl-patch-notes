**Game Update: Version 2025.11 Patch Notes**

---

### General Gameplay Changes

- **Plex Streaming Update:** 
  - **Feature Removal:** Free remote streaming access has been nerfed for all players. Roku users will feel the first sting of this change, effective immediately. Please update your in-game wallet for continued access or find a local streaming buddy.

- **Economy Adjustments:**
  - **GPU Prices:** Good news, gamers! GPU prices have been rebalanced to be more earth-bound, making your dream build slightly more achievable.
  - **RAM Prices:** In a twist of fate, RAM costs have skyrocketed, now 3x their previous value. Building a new PC might now feel like launching into space without a budget.

### Space Exploration & Emergency Protocols

- **China's Space Program:**
  - **New Feature:** Emergency Lifeboat Deployment. China has successfully implemented an emergency lifeboat system for astronauts. This feature ensures a swift return to Earth, showcasing a new level of efficiency in space emergency management. Players can now simulate emergency returns with this update.

### Housing & Economy

- **RealPage Settlement:**
  - **System Change:** The rent-setting algorithm has been gutted due to a settlement. Landlords can no longer use this tool for collusion, aiming to level the playing field in the housing market. Players might see a slight decrease in rental costs, but don't hold your breath.

- **Stamp Duty Proposal:**
  - **Suggestion:** A community suggestion to scrap stamp duty for those earning £60,000 looking for bigger homes has been added to the wishlist. No changes yet, but keep voting in-game for potential future updates.

### Gaming Hardware

- **Valve's Steam Machine:**
  - **Pricing Clarification:** Don't let the console-like appearance fool you; the Steam Machine will cost you as much as a high-end PC. Adjust your expectations and savings accordingly.

### Science & Exploration

- **Icy Moon Oceans:**
  - **Environmental Physics Update:** New research shows that forming oceans on icy moons can lead to boiling water scenarios due to unique pressure dynamics. Players exploring these moons will now encounter boiling water hazards.

- **Mushroom Foraging:**
  - **New Content:** Foragers can now collect 160 different species of mushrooms for various in-game uses like food, medicine, art, and science. This adds depth to survival and crafting mechanics.

### AI & Technology

- **Anthropic's Opus 4.5:**
  - **Upgrade:** A new, more efficient AI model has been released, allowing for longer, more engaging chats. This update addresses long-standing player feedback on interaction limits.

### Economic Policies

- **Minimum Wage Increase:**
  - **Economic Adjustment:** The minimum wage has been bumped up to £12.71 for over 21s. While welcomed, some in-game economists argue it still doesn't cover the cost of living in this virtual world.

- **Help to Save Expansion:**
  - **Feature Expansion:** The Help to Save scheme has been expanded to encourage more low-income players to save, with a 50% bonus after 2 and 4 years. Start saving to see the benefits!

### Political & Legal Updates

- **Justice System Overhaul:**
  - **Trial Mechanics:** Jury trials have been removed for all but the most serious cases (murder, rape, manslaughter) to reduce court backlogs. This change affects how legal challenges are resolved in-game.

- **Farage's Controversy:**
  - **Character Backstory Update:** Nigel Farage's character has received a controversial backstory update regarding past behavior. This might affect his interactions and reputation within the game.
