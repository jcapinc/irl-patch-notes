**Global Update v2025.12 Patch Notes - "The Year of the Unexpected"**

**General Updates:**

- **Word of the Year Update:** Merriam-Webster has released a new word update, crowning "slop" as the Word of the Year. This term now officially describes the low-quality AI content flooding the internet. Players can now use 'slop' to describe any content that feels like it was generated by an AI on a bad day. Patch includes new dialogue options and text generation features that might occasionally produce slop.

- **Security Enhancements:** Microsoft has finally patched the notorious RC4 cipher, which was like the boogeyman of encryption for decades. This update removes the ability for hackers to exploit this cipher in administrative authentication, effectively nerfing their favorite exploit. Say goodbye to easy hacks, hello to slightly more secure admin logins!

- **Economic Shift:** Ford has decided to end production of the F-150 Lightning, shifting focus to a new battery storage business. This economic pivot means players can no longer purchase new F-150 Lightnings in-game, but can now invest in battery storage solutions for an eco-friendly gameplay experience.

- **Content Moderation:** Google is discontinuing its dark web reports feature, citing it as "not helpful." This means players will no longer receive alerts about their data being leaked on the dark web, simplifying the game's notification system but potentially increasing the challenge of data security.

- **Cultural Expansion:** The UK has introduced a new policy encouraging Apple and Google to implement nudity-blocking algorithms on mobile devices. This update adds a new layer of content filtering, ensuring that unexpected NSFW content is blurred out, enhancing the family-friendly aspect of the game.
