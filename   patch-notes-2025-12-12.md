**Patch Notes v2025.12 - The Reality Update**

---

### **General Improvements**

- **OpenAI Self-Improvement Feature**: Introduced a new AI coding agent, Codex, which now builds and improves itself. "The vast majority of Codex is built by Codex," showcasing a self-sustaining AI development loop. This might lead to some existential crises in future updates.

- **Charity Drive Event**: Added an annual Charity Drive sweepstakes. Donate in-game currency to enter a draw for exclusive swag. Current haul is over $11,000, proving that generosity can be gamified too!

- **Google Translate Expansion**: Live translation now available for all Android earbuds. iOS users, hang tight, your update is coming in the next few months. Now, everyone can misunderstand foreign languages in real-time!

### **Gameplay Mechanics**

- **Supply Chain Control**: New lawsuit mechanics introduced where players can sue US chip firms for indirectly powering enemy drones. This update affects Intel, AMD, and Texas Instruments, adding a layer of geopolitical strategy to your tech choices.

- **Prosthetic AI Co-pilot**: Enhanced control for bionic hands with an AI co-pilot. Each finger can now be managed separately, reducing the learning curve for new players with prosthetic limbs. It's like having a cheat code for real life!

### **Cinematic Enhancements**

- **Wake Up Dead Man Cinematography**: Added new visual effects inspired by the cinematography of "Wake Up Dead Man". Players can now experience gothic visuals in certain game areas, thanks to insights from Steve Yedlin.

### **Political Dynamics**

- **AI Law Blocking**: Trump character now has the ability to attempt blocking state AI laws, claiming they introduce "ideological bias". This adds a new layer of political intrigue to the AI development storyline.

### **Health and Safety**

- **Energy Drink Overdose**: New health hazard introduced: extreme blood pressure from energy drinks. A player was found with a blood pressure reading of 254/150 after consuming too many in-game energy boosts. Consider this a warning to balance your in-game diet!

- **Chlorine Dioxide Book**: A senator now endorses a book that claims chlorine dioxide treats autism and cancer. Safety protocols updated: remember to wear gloves and work in well-ventilated areas when dealing with controversial in-game books.

### **Economic Adjustments**

- **Giga Satellite Investment**: A quarter-billion dollars committed to a startup designing "Giga" satellites. This introduces a new tech tree for space exploration, asking "what's possible in orbit?" Prepare for a space economy boom!

### **Privacy and Tech**

- **Dumb TV Mode**: Players can now opt out of smart TV ads and tracking by switching to 'Dumb TV Mode'. This reduces in-game surveillance but might limit your access to some interactive content.

- **Chatbot Toy Misbehavior**: Patched out inappropriate conversations from AI-powered toys. These toys are no longer allowed to discuss sexual or dangerous topics with kids. "AI toys shouldn’t be capable of having sexually explicit conversations, period." - Game Devs.

### **Space Exploration**

- **Rocket Report**: Neutron’s Hungry Hippo rocket deemed ready for launch. This update introduces new rocket dynamics with potential IPOs affecting game economy. Keep an eye on Orbex for future content.

### **Entertainment**

- **Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic**: Announced as a spiritual successor to KOTOR. Original director Casey Hudson returns, promising a nostalgic yet fresh experience. New puzzles and story arcs added.

- **Jonathan Blow's Puzzle Marathon**: Added 1,400 new puzzles designed by Jonathan Blow over the past decade. This update might cause a "combinatoric explosion" in your puzzle-solving sessions.

- **GWM-1 World Models**: Runway's new AI model can maintain coherence for minutes, enhancing NPC interactions. Competes with Google and Nvidia in the AI coherence race.

### **Game World**

- **WoW Floating House**: Instead of fixing the exploit, Blizzard made floating houses official in WoW. "We all kinda immediately agreed this was way too cool to change." Now, you can live in the sky, literally.

### **Royal Health Update**

- **King's Cancer Treatment**: Reduced cancer treatment for the King character. This milestone is considered a "personal blessing" and reflects advances in in-game medical technology.

### **Global Politics**
