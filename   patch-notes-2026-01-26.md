### Tech & Security Enhancements

- **Microsoft Traffic Rerouting Glitch**: A bug in Microsoft's network caused the domain 'example.com' to be rerouted to a server in Japan. - **Disk Encryption Without Microsoft Keys**: Players can now encrypt their PC disks without giving the recovery keys to Microsoft, ensuring privacy in the game's virtual environment. This update adds a new skill, 'Self-Reliant Encryption', under the cybersecurity tree.

- **AirTag 2 Upgrade**: Apple's AirTag 2 now features an improved chip for better tracking range and a louder speaker, making it easier for players to locate their lost items in-game. This is the first major update since the original AirTag, enhancing the 'Find My' gameplay mechanics.
