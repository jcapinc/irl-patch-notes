**Patch Notes Update: Version 2025.12.25**

### New Features & Content Updates

- **Santa Claus 24/7 Mode**: 
  - Santa Claus has been updated to a 24/7/365 operational mode. Players are now reminded that any misbehavior can result in a loss of festive magic, potentially breaking immersion. Please keep your conduct festive and magical at all times.

- **TV Technica: Seasonal Content Update**:
  - Added new seasonal content with 'Our Favorite Shows of 2025'. Netflix and Apple TV+ now dominate the in-game entertainment hub with new genres like thrillers, fantasy, sci-fi, and murder mysteries. Enjoy binge-watching sessions with improved streaming quality.

- **AI Coding Agents**:
  - New tutorial added: "How AI Coding Agents Work". Learn about compression tricks and multi-agent teamwork to enhance your in-game coding abilities. Remember, misuse can lead to unexpected bugs in your reality.

- **Christmas Carol Concert Event**:
  - Special event added: "Catherine and Charlotte Piano Duet". A pre-recorded performance featuring a duet by Catherine and Charlotte, playing a piece by Scottish composer Erland Cooper, now available on the in-game ITV1 channel.

- **Football League Christmas Day Fixture**:
  - Commemorative event for the 60th anniversary since the last Football League match on Christmas Day in England. Players can relive historical matches with enhanced nostalgia graphics.

- **JBL Bar 500MK2 Sound System**:
  - New in-game item: JBL Bar 500MK2. This compact 2.1 soundbar system introduces realistic Dolby Atmos surround sound, enhancing your gaming audio experience.

- **Work From Home Gift Pack**:
  - Added 25 new gift items under the 'Work From Home' category. These upgrades make your virtual office more fun and functional, including quirky desk accessories to boost productivity and morale.

- **All-Access AI Agents**:
  - Introduction of 'All-Access AI Agents'. These agents now have more personal interaction capabilities, but beware, they might know too much about your in-game private life.

### Gameplay Adjustments

- **SPEED Act Implementation**:
  - The gameplay environment has been altered with the passing of the SPEED Act, reducing the scope of the environmental review process. This might lead to faster project completions but at the potential cost of in-game ecosystem balance.

- **Demilitarised Zones in Ukraine**:
  - New gameplay option: 'Free Economic Zone' in the Donbas region. Players can now explore economic strategies instead of military ones in this area, offering a peaceful alternative to conflict.

- **Flu Dynamics Update**:
  - Flu cases have been adjusted to decrease before Christmas, providing a less challenging health environment during festive gameplay. However, a bounce-back mechanic has been added for the new year to keep players on their toes.

---

### Bug Fixes & Content Removal

- **Festive Swim Event Cancellation**:
  - Due to a weather system bug causing high winds, some festive swim events in Devon and Cornwall have been removed from the game schedule. Players are advised to avoid these areas or risk being stuck in a weather loop.

- **Misophonia Awareness**:
  - Fixed an issue where Christmas sounds were overly triggering for players with misophonia. New settings allow for sound customization to make festive periods more enjoyable for affected players.
