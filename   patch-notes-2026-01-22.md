### **Bug Fixes & Gameplay Adjustments:**

- **Hacker's Legal Status**: Fixed an issue where hackers could not legally transition into cybersecurity roles. Now, completing the "Path to Redemption" quest legally clears their in-game record.

- **Grok's Legal Loophole**: Addressed a glitch where victims of fake nudes had to sue in an unfavorable court. Now, players have a choice of jurisdiction when dealing with in-game legal disputes involving AI-generated content.

- **SMS Security Flaw**: Patched a vulnerability where sign-in links via SMS could expose player data. Now, all sensitive communications are encrypted and require two-factor authentication.

- **Police Force Reduction**: Adjusted the number of in-game police NPCs following the "largest reform in decades". This change affects how law enforcement interacts with players, potentially increasing the challenge of certain missions.

- **Oscar Nomination Surprises**: Updated the game's cultural events with new Oscar nomination outcomes. Players can now engage in discussions and events around the nominations, with special quests for films like "Sinners" and "Hamnet".

- **Trump's Peace Framework**: Introduced a satirical questline where players can attempt to sideline the UN with Trump's "Board of Peace". Critics within the game dismiss it as a vanity project, adding humor to diplomatic gameplay.

- **Nato Troop Misplacement**: Corrected an oversight where Nato troops were shown avoiding front lines in Afghanistan. Now, troop placement reflects historical accuracy, affecting war simulation outcomes.

- **Victorian Shoes Mystery**: Added a side quest where players can investigate the mystery of 200 Victorian shoes washing up on a beach. Solve the puzzle to earn "Historical Detective" badge.

- **Parenting Tips from Hostage Negotiator**: Implemented new parenting mechanics based on real-life hostage negotiation techniques. Use these to manage in-game child NPCs more effectively.

- **NFL Backup QB Story**: Updated the sports simulation to include a storyline where a backup QB, inactive for two years, leads his team to the Super Bowl. Players can take on this challenge for a thrilling sports narrative.

- **ICE Detention Issue**: Fixed an exploit where young NPCs could be detained with their parents. Now, the game reflects more humane policies, with specific missions to advocate for family unity.

- **Funeral Firm Collapse**: Added a financial cautionary tale with the collapse of Safe Hands Plans Ltd, teaching players about the risks of in-game investments.

- **Flag Tying Fatality**: A tragic reminder in the form of a public safety PSA within the game world, where players are warned about the dangers of climbing lampposts to tie flags.
