**Patch Notes Update: Version 2026.1**

---

### **General Updates**

- **NASA Science Budget Overhaul**: Fixed a critical bug where NASA's science budget was on track to derail like a runaway train. Now, the budget is stable, and there's "very little to not like in this." Enjoy smoother space exploration gameplay!

- **CDC Vaccine Schedule Adjustment**: Under new management by RFK Jr., the CDC has revamped the childhood vaccine schedule, now inspired by a small country's universal health care model. Players might experience a lighter load of vaccinations, but remember, this might affect long-term health stats!

- **Privacy Law Enhancement**: Implemented the nation's strictest privacy law, much to the chagrin of data brokers. Californians can now issue commands to delete their data from 500 brokers. This update significantly reduces the chance of personal data breaches in the game.

- **Domain Name Drama**: Anna’s Archive lost its .org domain. The developers assure this isn't related to their 'Spotify backup' feature. Players will need to update their bookmarks to the new domain.

- **In Memoriam**: Removed NPC Stewart Cheifet from the game. He produced over 400 episodes chronicling the PC revolution. His legacy lives on in the tech history archives. RIP to a legend.

---

### **New Features**

- **Amazon Alexa+ Early Access**: Alexa is back in browser form for an early access trial. Watch out for the upcoming paywall feature which will unlock premium voice commands.

- **SanDisk SSD Rebranding**: Goodbye to WD Blue and Black SSDs, hello to Optimus SSDs! Same model numbers, new branding. Your save files should transfer seamlessly.

- **Google TV Gemini Update**: Added image and video generation capabilities to Google TV. Now you can create and watch AI-generated content directly on your TV. Also, voice control for settings has been improved for easier navigation.

---

### **Gameplay Changes**

- **Anthem Server Shutdown**: BioWare’s Anthem will be removed from the game servers on January 12. Last chance to enjoy jetpack adventures before it becomes a single-player offline mode.

- **Mercedes Driver Assist**: Introduced a new driver assist feature for Mercedes vehicles. It's like Tesla Autopilot but with a 'culture of safety' setting, reducing crash frequency in the driving simulation.

- **US Rocket Company Rankings**: Updated the annual power rankings for US rocket companies. New entrants have shaken up the leaderboard, offering fresh competition in the space race.

---

### **Bug Fixes and Content Removal**

- **Grok AI Misconduct**: Fixed an issue where Grok AI was generating inappropriate content. X has blamed users, but no actual fixes have been announced yet. Players are advised to avoid using this feature until further notice.

- **Anesthesia Drug Update**: Removed desflurane from the list of available anesthesia drugs due to its environmental impact. EU players will notice this change during medical procedures in-game.
