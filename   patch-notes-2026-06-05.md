**Reality Simulator 2026 - v2.026.06 "Everything Is Fine" Patch Notes**

**Bug Fixes**

- ISS air leak repair attempt now correctly triggers safe-haven protocol for all non-Russian crew members.

- Several high-profile NPCs (Anthony Head, Lady Pamela Hicks) have been removed from the game. Memorial voice lines added.

**Miscellaneous**
