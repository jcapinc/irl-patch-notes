**Global Update v2026.02 - Patch Notes**

---

### **New Features:**

- **Dinosaur Eggshell Dating System (v1.0)**
  - Added new feature: Dinosaur eggshells now serve as isotopic time capsules, allowing players to date other fossils with increased accuracy. This feature enhances the gameplay of paleontologists by adding a layer of precision to fossil dating. [Source](https://arstechnica.com/science/2026/02/dinosaur-eggshells-can-reveal-the-age-of-other-fossils/)

- **Artemis II Moon Mission (v2.1)**
  - Updated launch window: The Artemis II mission now targets March 6 for its earliest launch attempt. Players should note that there are still pending tasks before the launch, adding an element of anticipation and suspense to the game. [Source](https://arstechnica.com/space/2026/02/nasa-reports-no-significant-leaks-in-artemis-ii-fueling-test-eyes-march-6-launch/)

- **Giant Tortoise Reintroduction (v1.0)**
  - New environment addition: 158 juvenile giant tortoises have been reintroduced to a Galápagos island, previously extinct since the 1800s. This update brings back a beloved species to the game's ecosystem, enhancing biodiversity and player exploration. [Source](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9wn1jrzk4go)

### **Gameplay Mechanics Changes:**

- **Genetic Testing Leap (v1.0)**
  - Introduced new mechanic: Players can now engage with commercial genetic testing, though with a caveat - the impact on human diversity is still under review. This feature might lead to unexpected gameplay changes in future updates. [Source](https://arstechnica.com/science/2026/02/have-we-leapt-into-commercial-genetic-testing-without-understanding-it/)

- **Global Tariff Increase (v15.0)**
  - Economic gameplay adjustment: Trump's tariffs have been outlawed, but new tariffs at 15% have been introduced. This affects trade dynamics, making resource management more challenging for players. [Source](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8z48xwqn3o)

---

### **Balance Changes:**

- **Foreign Scientist Access (v0.9)**
  - Gameplay restriction: Noncitizen scientists at NIST labs have lost after-hours access, affecting research speed for players choosing this faction. This change aims to balance the game by reducing the advantage of international collaboration. [Source](https://arstechnica.com/science/2026/02/major-government-research-lab-appears-to-be-squeezing-out-foreign-scientists/)

- **UK Troop Deployment (v1.0)**
  - Strategy update: UK players can now deploy non-combat troops to Ukraine in peaceful areas, adding a new strategic layer without direct combat. This might influence diplomatic relations and game alliances. [Source](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp32n6vxqplo)
