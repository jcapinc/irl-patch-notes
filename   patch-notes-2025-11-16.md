- **AI Life Organization:** Google Gemini and ChatGPT can now schedule actions, helping players organize their in-game life more efficiently. (Source: [WIRED](https://www.wired.com/story/google-gemini-and-chatgpt-scheduled-actions/))

- **Smart Home Upgrade:** Players can now ditch Alexa for a more robust, cloud-independent smart home system, reducing downtime and increasing reliability. (Source: [WIRED](https://www.wired.com/story/heres-how-i-ditched-alexa-and-upgraded-my-smart-home/))
