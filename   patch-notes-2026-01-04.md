### **New Features and Gameplay Mechanics**

- **Authoritarian Power Precedent**: Trump's latest move introduces a new game mechanic where players can now set precedents for authoritarian powers. This feature might unlock new global leader abilities, but watch out, as it could also trigger a chain reaction of similar moves from other players worldwide. [Source: Jeremy Bowen Analysis]

- **Venezuela Conquest Mode**: A new campaign mode has been added where players can simulate US military actions in Venezuela. Key questions include how to seize control and manage the aftermath, with new mini-games for capturing political figures. [Source: Ros Atkins Analysis]

- **Maduro Capture Quest**: Follow a detailed questline with visual aids to capture Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro. This includes interactive maps and photos for a more immersive experience. [Source: Caracas to New York Operation]

- **Political Turncoat Mechanic**: Delcy Rodríguez, previously loyal to Maduro, can now switch sides to work for Trump. This introduces a new dynamic where loyalty can be bought, adding depth to political strategy gameplay. [Source: Delcy Rodríguez's New Role]

- **Danish Sovereignty Defense**: A new diplomatic challenge where players can engage in a scenario where the US attempts to annex Greenland. Danish PM Mette Frederiksen has introduced a 'No Annexation' policy, affecting international relations gameplay. [Source: Danish PM's Statement]

- **Weather Impact Expansion**: More snow and ice now significantly affect gameplay, especially in the UK, with new challenges for school and work commutes post-Christmas. Players must strategize around these conditions. [Source: UK Weather Warning]

- **Health Challenges for NPCs**: A new emotional storyline where Jesy Nelson's character deals with the heartbreaking news that her twin babies might never walk, introducing deeper narrative elements into family dynamics within the game. [Source: Jesy Nelson's Personal Story]

- **Migrant Surveillance Update**: New power to seize phones from migrant NPCs to gather intelligence on smuggling gangs. This feature adds a layer of espionage and security gameplay. [Source: New Migrant Policy]

- **Historical Figure Passing**: A tribute event for Auschwitz survivor Eva Schloss has been added, where players can attend a virtual memorial, enhancing the game's educational aspect. [Source: Eva Schloss' Passing]

- **Environmental Activism**: Players can now participate in actions like the Berlin fire by the Vulkan Gruppe, aimed at disrupting the fossil fuel economy, introducing eco-warfare scenarios. [Source: Berlin Power Cut]

- **Parliament Maintenance Mode**: A new economic simulation where UK MPs must decide on funding for Parliament's maintenance, affecting political stability and gameplay resources. [Source: Parliament Maintenance]

- **Post-Christmas Blues**: A new psychological mechanic where players must manage post-holiday depression when returning to work, with advice from in-game career coaches to ease the transition. [Source: Post-Christmas Return Guide]

- **Pet Winter Care**: New mini-games focused on caring for pets in cold weather, adding to the life simulation aspect of the game. [Source: Pet Care in Cold Weather]

- **Junk Food Advertising Ban**: A new policy simulation where players can see the effects of banning junk food ads, potentially leading to healthier NPC populations and new market strategies for food companies. [Source: Junk Food Ad Ban]

- **Venezuela Oil Strategy**: A complex economic strategy game where players as Trump must navigate the challenges of extracting oil from Venezuela, balancing international law, local politics, and economic investment. [Source: Trump's Oil Strategy]

- **Cilia Flores Capture**: Adds a subplot where players can engage in capturing Cilia Flores, providing insights into political power dynamics and corruption within the game. [Source: Cilia Flores Profile]

- **Florida's 'Little Venezuela'**: Celebrate or strategize in this new area affected by Venezuelan politics, with mixed emotions of joy and uncertainty influencing local gameplay. [Source: Florida's Little Venezuela]
